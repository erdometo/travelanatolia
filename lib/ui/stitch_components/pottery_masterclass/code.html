The Experience

Step into a cavernous workshop carved directly from the soft volcanic rock of Cappadocia. Here, the red clay of the Kızılırmak River has been shaped by human hands since the era of the Hittites. This is not just a class; it is a profound dialogue with the earth and history.

Under the guidance of a master artisan, you will learn the rhythmic, almost meditative process of operating a traditional kick-wheel. Feel the cool, raw clay transform beneath your fingers as you are guided through techniques passed down through generations. Whether you craft a simple bowl or attempt an intricate wine jug, the piece you create will hold the unique fingerprint of your journey in Anatolia.

Your Host

Master Galip

A sixth-generation potter whose hands hold the secrets of Avanos clay. Galip speaks little English, but his warm laughter and patient gestures transcend language, making every guest feel like family.
